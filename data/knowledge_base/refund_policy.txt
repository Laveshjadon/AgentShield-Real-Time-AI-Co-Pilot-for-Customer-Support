REFUND AND RETURN POLICY
========================

1. Refund Eligibility
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase if:
- The product is defective or damaged on arrival
- The product does not match the description on the website
- The product stopped working within the first 30 days

2. How to Claim a Refund
To process a refund, ask the customer to provide:
- Their original Order ID (found in the confirmation email)
- A brief description of the problem
- Photos of the defective product (if applicable)

3. Refund Timeline
- Refunds are processed within 2-3 business days after approval
- The amount is credited back to the original payment method
- Bank transfers may take 5-7 additional business days to appear

4. Non-Refundable Items
The following items are NOT eligible for a refund:
- Digital downloads or software licenses once activated
- Customized or personalized products
- Products returned after 30 days without prior approval
- Items damaged due to customer misuse

5. Exchange Policy
If the customer prefers an exchange over a refund:
- The replacement will be shipped within 3-5 business days
- The defective item must be returned within 14 days
- No extra charge for the replacement if it is the same product

6. Partial Refunds
A partial refund (50%) may be issued if:
- The product is returned after 30 days but before 60 days
- The product shows signs of use but is not defective
