# Horizon Federal Bank: Frontline Banking FAQ

**Classification:** Synthetic retrieval dataset

## FAQ-001: UPI Debited but Failed

**Question:** My account was debited but the UPI payment failed.  
**Answer:** Register the complaint and communicate the system-calculated deadline under the applicable RBI failed-transaction rule. Do not guarantee the posting time.  
**Agent action:** Capture transaction ID, date, amount, and beneficiary; provide `[Ticket ID]`.  
**Escalation:** Prescribed reversal deadline breached.  
**Source:** RBI failed-transaction TAT framework.

## FAQ-002: Unauthorized Card Transaction

**Question:** I do not recognize a card transaction.  
**Answer:** Secure the card, register the unauthorized transaction, preserve the reporting timestamp, and explain that liability depends on the cause and reporting conditions.  
**Escalation:** Continuing activity, account takeover, or multi-channel fraud.  
**Source:** RBI customer-liability circular dated July 6, 2017.

## FAQ-003: ATM Debited but No Cash

**Question:** The ATM debited my account but did not dispense cash.  
**Answer:** Register the ATM dispute. The RBI framework prescribes proactive reversal within T+5 days and compensation for delay beyond that period.  
**Required information:** ATM ID/location, amount, date, time, transaction reference.

## FAQ-004: KYC Update

**Question:** How can I update my KYC?  
**Answer:** Use the authenticated digital, branch, or other approved channel shown for the account. Never send OTPs or passwords with KYC documents.  
**Source:** Current RBI KYC Master Direction.

## FAQ-005: Missed EMI

**Question:** What should I do if my EMI failed?  
**Answer:** Check the account-specific reason, contractual due amount, approved payment methods, and available assistance. Do not assume a universal grace period.  
**Escalation:** Financial distress, mandate error, disputed charge, or possible bank error.

## FAQ-006: Foreclosure Charges

**Question:** Is there a foreclosure charge?  
**Answer:** It depends on the loan type, rate type, borrower category, agreement, and applicable regulation. Generate an account-specific foreclosure statement.  
**Prohibited response:** “All floating loans have no charge.”

## FAQ-007: Original Property Documents

**Question:** When will my original documents be returned?  
**Answer:** For covered personal loans, applicable RBI instructions require release and charge removal within 30 days after full repayment or settlement, subject to scope and effective-date conditions.  
**Escalation:** Deadline exceeded or documents lost/damaged.

## FAQ-008: Merchant Refund Missing

**Question:** The merchant says a refund was processed, but I have not received it.  
**Answer:** Collect the merchant refund reference and expected date, trace the credit, and register a dispute if eligible. Merchant refund and chargeback are different processes.

## FAQ-009: Complaint to RBI

**Question:** Can I complain to RBI?  
**Answer:** Customers may use the RBI Integrated Ombudsman route after first complaining to the regulated entity and satisfying the scheme's eligibility conditions.  
**Official portal:** https://cms.rbi.org.in/

## FAQ-010: Information the Bank Will Never Request

**Question:** Will the bank ask for my OTP or PIN?  
**Answer:** No legitimate support agent should ask for an OTP, PIN, CVV, password, or complete card number.
