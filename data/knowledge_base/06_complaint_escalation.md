# Horizon Federal Bank: Customer Complaint and Escalation Framework

**Classification:** Synthetic internal demo policy

## Complaint Principles

Every complaint must receive:

- A clear category
- A timestamped complaint date
- A reference number
- An owner or queue
- A verified response target
- Documented customer updates

## Escalation Levels

### Level 1 - Frontline

Complete available actions, register the complaint, explain verified expectations, and provide `[Ticket ID]`.

### Level 2 - Specialist Operations

Use for transaction tracing, loan servicing, KYC exceptions, card disputes, technical incidents, and policy interpretation outside frontline authority.

### Level 3 - Nodal Complaint Handling

Use for repeated failures, serious service deficiency, regulatory-risk complaints, unresolved high-impact cases, or cases reaching the configured escalation threshold.

### Fraud Investigation Unit

Use for account takeover, continuing unauthorized activity, multi-channel fraud, insider concern, or specialist liability investigation.

## RBI Integrated Ombudsman

**Verified regulatory requirement**

A customer may complain under the applicable RBI Integrated Ombudsman framework after first complaining to the regulated entity and meeting the scheme's eligibility conditions. Frontline agents must not say every complaint is immediately eligible.

Provide the official RBI Complaint Management System route when the case qualifies. Do not discourage lawful escalation.

## Repeat Complaint Handling

Do not create disconnected tickets for the same unresolved issue. Link prior references, preserve the original complaint date, and escalate the complete history.

## Suggested Script - Adapt Naturally

> I have registered your complaint under `[Category]`. Your reference is `[Ticket ID]`, and the verified response target is `[Target Date]`. If the matter is not resolved at this level, the same complaint record will be escalated so you do not need to repeat the full history.

## Prohibited Actions

- Closing a complaint without evidence
- Changing the complaint date to improve SLA metrics
- Telling the customer not to contact RBI
- Promising a named officer's callback without system assignment
