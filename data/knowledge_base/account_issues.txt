ACCOUNT & LOGIN ISSUES POLICY

Q: Customer cannot log in to their account.
A: Troubleshooting steps in order:
1. Ask customer to check if they are using the correct email/mobile number registered with us.
2. Try "Forgot Password" flow — OTP will be sent to registered mobile.
3. If OTP not received: check if the number is active and not in DND. Resend OTP after 30 seconds.
4. If account is locked (5 failed attempts), it auto-unlocks after 30 minutes. Agent can also manually unlock from CRM.
5. If login still fails, escalate to tech support with customer ID.

Q: Customer says their account has been deactivated.
A: Accounts are deactivated for inactivity (no login for 12 months) or due to a compliance flag.
- Inactivity deactivation: Customer must submit a reactivation request with KYC verification. Process takes 2–3 business days.
- Compliance flag: Do NOT discuss reasons on call. Transfer to the compliance team immediately.

Q: Customer wants to change their registered mobile number.
A: Mobile number change requires:
1. OTP verification on the OLD number.
2. If old number is inaccessible, customer must visit the nearest branch with government-issued photo ID.
3. Changes reflect within 24 hours after verification.

Q: Customer says they did not make a transaction shown in their account.
A: This is a potential fraud case. Steps:
1. Immediately ask the customer to block their card via the app or by calling the card blocking helpline.
2. Raise a fraud dispute ticket — category "unauthorized transaction".
3. Advise the customer NOT to share OTP or password with anyone.
4. Provisional credit may be issued within 10 business days while investigation is ongoing.
5. Do not confirm whether fraud occurred — only the fraud team can confirm.

CARD MANAGEMENT

Q: How to block a debit/credit card?
A: Customer can block card via: app > Cards > Block Card. Or agent can block from CRM > Customer Profile > Card Management > Block. Temporary block takes effect immediately. Permanent block (if card is lost/stolen) requires a new card request.

Q: How long does a replacement card take?
A: Standard delivery is 7–10 business days. Express delivery (additional fee) is 2–3 business days. Customer will receive SMS when card is dispatched.

Q: Card declined at POS / ATM. What to check?
A: Possible reasons:
- Daily transaction limit exceeded (default: ₹1,00,000/day for debit)
- International transactions not enabled
- Card expired
- Incorrect PIN entered 3 times (card temporarily blocked for 24 hours)
- Merchant category blocked by customer's own settings
Check card status in CRM and inform the customer of the specific reason.
