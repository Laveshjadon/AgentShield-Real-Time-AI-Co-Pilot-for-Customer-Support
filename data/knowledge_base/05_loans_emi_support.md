# Horizon Federal Bank: Loans and EMI Support Guide

**Classification:** Synthetic internal demo policy

## EMI Payment Failure

An EMI may fail because of insufficient funds, an expired or rejected mandate, account restriction, or technical error.

**Agent action**

- Confirm the loan account using masked identifiers.
- Check the verified payment status, due amount, mandate status, and contractual charges.
- Offer approved payment channels.
- Do not invent a grace period or promise that credit reporting will not occur.
- For financial distress, route the customer to the authorized assistance or restructuring team.

## Penal Charges

Agents must quote only charges shown in the signed loan agreement and current schedule of charges. Do not capitalize penal charges as interest or describe a discretionary waiver as guaranteed.

## Prepayment and Foreclosure

- Retrieve the account-specific rate type and agreement.
- Explain that charges depend on the product, borrower category, rate type, funding source, and applicable regulation.
- Generate a formal foreclosure statement rather than verbally estimating the settlement amount.
- Never claim all floating-rate loans are identical without checking the applicable rule and agreement.

## Interest-Rate Disputes

Create an interest-dispute request containing the agreed rate, current rate, reset date, benchmark, spread, statement period, and customer evidence. Frontline agents cannot authorize a rate correction or refund.

## Loan Closure and Original Documents

**Verified regulatory requirement**

For covered personal loans, RBI's responsible-lending instructions require regulated entities to release original movable or immovable property documents and remove registered charges within 30 days after full repayment or settlement. Delay attributable to the regulated entity attracts compensation of Rs 5,000 per day. Additional treatment applies where documents are lost or damaged.

Use the regulation's scope and effective-date conditions before promising compensation.

## Credit Information

Do not estimate how many points a missed payment will reduce a credit score. Record a credit-information dispute where repayment, closure, or delinquency reporting appears incorrect.

## Suggested Script - Adapt Naturally

> I understand that an EMI issue can be stressful. I will first confirm whether this was a mandate failure, a technical issue, or an unpaid instalment. I will then explain the options shown for your specific loan agreement. Your service reference is `[Ticket ID]`.
