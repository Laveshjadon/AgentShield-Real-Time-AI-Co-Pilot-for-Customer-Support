Welcome to TechNova Support!
Below is our general refund and troubleshooting policy.

1. Refund Policy
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase if the product is defective or does not meet their expectations.
To claim a refund, the customer must provide their original order ID and a brief reason for the return.
Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

2. Internet Connectivity Issues
If a customer complains about their internet not working, ask them to check the router lights. 
If the "Internet" light is blinking red, it means there is an outage in their area.
If the light is solid green but there is no internet, ask the customer to unplug the router, wait 30 seconds, and plug it back in.

3. Account Lockouts
If a customer is locked out of their account due to too many failed password attempts, they must wait 15 minutes before trying again.
Agents can manually send a password reset link to the email on file. Agents should never ask for the customer's current password over the phone.
