# Horizon Federal Bank: KYC and Account Services Guide

**Classification:** Synthetic internal demo policy

## Scope

This guide covers periodic KYC, address and name changes, account restrictions, dormant accounts, account closure, nominees, joint accounts, minor accounts, and deceased-customer servicing.

## Data-Minimization Rule

Agents must collect only information required by the approved workflow. Never ask a customer to disclose an OTP, PIN, CVV, banking password, or full Aadhaar number during a support interaction.

## Periodic KYC

**Verified regulatory requirement**

Follow the current RBI KYC Master Direction and the customer's risk category. Where no KYC information has changed, use an accepted self-declaration channel when permitted. For address-only changes, follow the current self-declaration and verification process.

**Agent action**

- Check the account's KYC status and configured due date.
- Explain accepted channels without requesting documents in an insecure channel.
- Generate `[Service Request ID]`.
- Escalate identity mismatch, suspected impersonation, sanctions, or AML alerts.

## Account Detail Changes

### Address Change

Use only approved digital, branch, or registered-contact channels. Do not promise immediate completion when positive verification is required.

### Name or Date-of-Birth Correction

Require the documents listed by the configured workflow. Frontline agents may create the request but cannot override documentary mismatch.

## Dormant or Restricted Accounts

Explain the restriction reason only to the verified account holder. Provide the approved reactivation route and avoid suggesting workarounds. Fraud, AML, legal, lien, and court restrictions require specialist review.

## Nominee and Deceased-Customer Requests

- Do not disclose balances or transactions to an unverified third party.
- Record claimant relationship and route to the deceased-claims workflow.
- Required documents and settlement treatment depend on nomination, account mandate, claim value, and applicable law.
- Never promise settlement before legal and operations review.

## Suggested Script - Adapt Naturally

> I can help you begin the KYC update. For your protection, please do not send identity documents through an unapproved channel. I have created request `[Service Request ID]`; the system will show the secure submission method and any verification still required.
