# Horizon Federal Bank: Frontline Conversation Playbook

**Classification:** Synthetic internal demo policy

## Conversation Sequence

1. Acknowledge the customer's reason for contacting.
2. Complete the required identity verification.
3. Restate the issue and confirm important facts.
4. Perform available actions before discussing escalation.
5. Explain verified timelines and dependencies.
6. Provide reference numbers.
7. Confirm the next step and close respectfully.

## Approved Language

### Opening

> Thank you for contacting Horizon Federal Bank. I understand you are calling about `[Issue]`. I will check this carefully and explain the next available step.

### Hold

> May I place you on hold for up to `[Verified Duration]` while I check the transaction? If I need longer, I will return with an update.

### Uncertain Outcome

> I can register and escalate the request today, but the final decision belongs to the authorized review team. I do not want to promise an outcome before that review is complete.

### Delay

> I am sorry this has taken longer than expected. The original complaint date remains `[Date]`, and I am escalating the existing case under reference `[Ticket ID]`.

### Hinglish

> Main samajh sakta hoon ki yeh situation stressful hai. Main pehle transaction ka verified status check karta hoon, phir aapko exact next step aur reference number bataunga.

> Main refund guarantee nahi kar sakta, lekin aapki complaint `[Ticket ID]` ke under register ho gayi hai aur applicable team review karegi.

## Prohibited Language

- “Calm down.”
- “This is your fault.”
- “You will definitely get the money today.”
- “I will personally fix it.”
- “Share your OTP so I can verify you.”
- “That is another department's problem.”

## Angry or Abusive Customers

Distinguish frustration from abuse. A customer criticizing service is not automatically toxic. Use the approved warning and call-termination workflow only for repeated personal abuse, threats, or safety risk.

## AgentShield Naming

AgentShield is internal. Say “our system” or “our banking platform” to customers.
