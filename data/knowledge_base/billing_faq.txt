BILLING AND PAYMENT FAQ
========================

1. How to Check Current Bill
Ask the customer to:
- Log into their account at www.yourcompany.com/account
- Click on "My Bills" in the left menu
- The latest invoice will be shown at the top

2. Payment Methods Accepted
We accept the following payment methods:
- Credit cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- Debit cards with Visa or Mastercard logo
- Net Banking (all major Indian banks)
- UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
- EMI (available for bills above Rs 5000, only on select credit cards)

3. Bill Payment Due Date
- Bills are generated on the 1st of every month
- Payment due date is the 15th of every month
- A late payment fee of Rs 100 is charged after the due date
- Service may be suspended after 30 days of non-payment

4. Late Payment Fee Waiver
An agent can waive the late payment fee ONCE per customer per year if:
- The customer has been with the company for more than 1 year
- The customer has a good payment history (no more than 2 late payments in 12 months)
- The customer provides a valid reason (travel, hospitalization, etc.)
- Approval is not required from supervisor for amounts below Rs 200

5. Bill Dispute Process
If the customer disputes a charge on their bill:
Step 1: Verify the charge by checking the account notes in the CRM
Step 2: If the charge looks incorrect, raise a dispute ticket immediately
Step 3: Inform the customer that the dispute will be resolved within 7 working days
Step 4: If the dispute is valid, a credit will be added to the next bill
Step 5: Never promise a cash refund for billing disputes without supervisor approval

6. Duplicate Payment
If a customer has paid twice:
- Verify both payments in the payment gateway portal
- Raise a duplicate payment ticket with both transaction IDs
- The excess amount will be refunded within 10-15 business days
- Alternatively, the excess can be adjusted against the next bill (faster option)

7. EMI Plans
For customers who want to pay in installments:
- EMI is available for outstanding amounts above Rs 5000
- Duration options: 3 months, 6 months, or 12 months
- No extra interest for 3-month EMI
- 1.5% monthly interest for 6 and 12 month EMI
- EMI setup requires verification of the registered credit card
